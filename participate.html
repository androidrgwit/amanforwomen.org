Terms and conditions

Awards will be decided by a committee composed of members of A Man for Women, those donors who wish to participate in the selection of their respective awards, and volunteers who would agree to help on the selection based on the criteria established by A Man for Women.

Only one prize per participant will be awarded. If multiple prizes are awarded they may be ranked accordingly, such as first prize, second prize, et cetera. No prizes will be awarded for proposals that do not reach a minimum of six participants, or for proposals that are not well documented. Awards not granted will be passed to the following year's competition or kept for other activities of A Man for Women.

Decisions made by the award committee will be based on the work done and the evidence provided. The decision of the award committee is final. Prize winners will be contacted to determine the preferred way of receiving the prize.

The participants agree that A Man for Women foundation uses the material submitted in the competition for future activities of the foundation. Names of winners will be published in the web page of A Man for Women. As of August 2016, the Foundation agrees to fund the awards in 2017 and 2018. Further prizes will be decided upon results obtained on participation and donors' commitment.

The participant is responsible for the authenticity of the material submitted and has the required authorisations to take the pictures.
